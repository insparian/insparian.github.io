Recently I have been checking out digital nomad visas (DNV) in order to move myself to a more-desired location. In the mean time, I want the benifits of possibility getting a permanant residency. 

I don't care where you read that DNV in Malta will give you the rights to naturalise, this piece of info is absolutly WRONG! The person said this is possible did not read all information on the offical website carefully. It is possible to apply for a permant resident permit and reannounce the nomad resident permit through the [agency](https://residencymalta.gov.mt/list-of-agents-residencymalta/).

[Offical website for DNV in Malta.](https://nomad.residencymalta.gov.mt/)

Eligibility: Non-EU nationals

Length: 1 year, renewable, max. 3 years

Application Fee: €300, non-refundable

Entry Visa Fee: check your local Malta embassy or commission

Premium Visa Service: €300 (door-to-door service, not avaliable in every country)

Following Approval in Principle (provide within 30 working days)
- Proof of accommodation: temprary accommodation of 1 month for the letter of final approval. Long-term accommodation covering the whole duration of the resident permit (min. 12 months) is needed for the resident card.
- Healthy insurance: local/international fully-comprehensive health insurance covering for one year, travel insurance policy is not accepted.
